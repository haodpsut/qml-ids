The Critical Challenge in Operational IDS

High Recall (Catching Attacks)

  • Classical LSTMs are great at identifying the vast majority of true attacks.
  • This minimizes the risk of missing a critical security incident (low False Negatives).
  • This is often reflected by a high F1-Score in academic benchmarks.

The Cost: Low Precision (False Alarms)

  • High recall often comes at the cost of misclassifying many benign activities as attacks.
  • This creates a high number of False Positives, leading to "alert fatigue" for SOC analysts.
  • Too many false alarms can cause real threats to be overlooked.

The Operational Goal:

Can we find a model that prioritizes high-fidelity alerts (high precision) to build a more trustworthy and efficient IDS, even if it means a slight drop in overall recall?
