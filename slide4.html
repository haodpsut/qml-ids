Our Approach: A Hybrid Quantum-Classical LSTM

Fig. 1. The proposed hybrid QLSTM architecture.

Part 1: Classical LSTM Feature Extractor

A standard LSTM processes sequential network data to extract a rich temporal feature vector (the hidden state).

Part 2: Quantum Classifier (PQC)

Instead of a classical MLP, we use a Parameterized Quantum Circuit (PQC) as the final classifier.

The Hypothesis

By leveraging the larger computational space of quantum mechanics, the PQC can learn a more precise and robust decision boundary, potentially leading to fewer false positives.
