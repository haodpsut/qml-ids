Conclusion

A Valuable Trade-off Uncovered

Our central finding is that hybrid QLSTM models offer a different, valuable performance profile: they consistently achieve superior precision at the cost of slightly lower recall compared to classical LSTMs.

The Practical Value of High Fidelity

This trade-off is crucial for real-world security operations. By significantly reducing false alarms (~29%), QML offers a path toward high-fidelity IDS that enhance analyst efficiency and trust.

Final Message

Quantum Machine Learning may not be a universal replacement for classical models, but it is a powerful tool to explore new performance profiles and trade-offs that are vital for practical applications.
