Experimental Setup

Dataset: CIC-IDS2017

A large-scale, realistic benchmark for IDS with over 2.8 million network flows.

We formulate this as a binary classification task: BENIGN vs. ATTACK.

Models for Comparison

Classical LSTM (Baseline)

LSTM feature extractor + classical MLP classifier.

Hybrid QLSTM (Ours)

LSTM feature extractor + Quantum (PQC) classifier. We test variants with 4 and 8 qubits.

Key Evaluation Metrics

Precision Recall F1-Score Accuracy

Focusing on the trade-off between Precision and Recall.
