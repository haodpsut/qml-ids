Our Primary Contributions

Hybrid QLSTM Design

We design and implement a novel hybrid architecture combining a classical LSTM for feature extraction with a PQC for classification in NIDS.

Uncovering a Critical Trade-off

Our central finding: the QLSTM model consistently achieves superior Precision, while the classical baseline excels in Recall.

Contextualizing Practical Value

We demonstrate the practical importance of this trade-off, highlighting a path toward high-fidelity IDS that reduce false alarms in real-world SOCs.
